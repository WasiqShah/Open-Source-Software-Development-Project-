Some universities allow you to construct your own major, perhaps in an interdisciplinary field. And at other universities, you're allowed to take a series of minors, rather than picking one single major. Such alternative paths can be great opportunities if you have a clear idea of what you want to do after graduation and the skills you will need to do it.
