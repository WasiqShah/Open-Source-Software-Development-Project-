  <h1>Tips For Choosing Your Majour</h1>


  <h3>
Is it the right time to declare a major? 
</h3>

  <p style="font-size:100%">
Never declare your majors in haste. Being motivated or influenced by another one to pursue that career might make you declare your major straight away. But always restrain form falling into that practice. Resist the temptation to sign on just because your other fellows are doing it or the adviser is pressuring you. On the other hand, if yours is a major with boatloads of requirements (like marketing or IT or a premed program)—and if you're 100 percent certain that this is what you really want to do (no if, ands, or buts)—it can be a good idea to declare right off the bat.

 
</p>
